# Amalanku Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: [August 17, 2025]_

Amalanku ("the App") is designed to help Muslims maintain consistency in their daily Islamic practices. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it with utmost respect for your privacy.

## üîç Data Collection

### What We Collect:

- **Minimal Data**: Amalanku is designed to collect as little data as possible and operates entirely offline
- **Amalan Records**: Your daily practice records (prayers, dhikr, Quran reading) stored locally on your device only
- **User Preferences**: Settings like notification times, reminder preferences, and app configurations (stored locally on your device)
- **Prayer Times**: Location-based prayer time calculations processed locally on your device

### What We _Don't_ Collect:

- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifying information
- **Usage Analytics**: We do not track how you use the app or collect usage statistics
- **Device Information**: Beyond basic requirements for app functionality
- **Account Data**: No user accounts or cloud storage - everything stays on your device
- **Behavioral Data**: We do not monitor or analyze your religious practices or spiritual activities

### Local Storage:

- **Purpose**: To save your amalan records, preferences, and app settings locally on your device
- **Location**: All data is stored locally on your device only - no cloud storage or external servers
- **Control**: You have complete control over your data and can delete it anytime through app settings

## üåê Permissions & Network Access

### Required Permissions:

- **Storage Permission**: Required to export your amalan records as PDF files for personal backup or sharing
- **Notification Permission**: Required to send prayer time reminders and amalan notifications
- **Location Permission (Optional)**: Used to calculate accurate prayer times for your area and to show nearby mosques; all processing respects your privacy
- **Internet Access**: Required only to fetch content from the Amalanku API (prayer times, doa, dzikir)
- **No Personal Data Sent**: The app does not transmit your personal info to the API


### What We _Don't_ Access:

- **Internet Access**: Amalanku works completely offline and does not require internet connection
- **Camera**: We do not access your camera
- **Contacts**: We do not access your contact list
- **Microphone**: We do not access your microphone
- **Phone**: We do not access phone functions or call logs

**Important**: All data processing happens locally on your device. No data is ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.
